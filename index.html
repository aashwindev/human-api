The agent era arrived in 2025 and immediately disappointed — not because the models were weak, but because they had no idea who they were acting for.

// 06 — THE ORGANIZATIONAL DIMENSION

    

      

        
When every person in an organization has a Human API, the organization becomes queryable.

        
Every company is building internal knowledge bases. Notion, Confluence, Slack archives, RAG over documents. The document layer is being solved.

        
The human layer is not. The actual people — their judgment, their lived experience, how they'd approach a problem, their specific way of thinking — is not queryable. It dies when they leave the company. Or when they're in a meeting and can't be reached. Or when the company grows past the point where everyone can know everyone.

        
Human API fixes this from the bottom up. Not a document system. A network of human minds, structured and queryable.

        
A new employee joins. Instead of 40 hours of onboarding calls, you tell the agent: use the Human API of the five people this person needs to learn from. The agent teaches them everything — in each person's specific style, with each person's specific judgment — in a fraction of the time.
